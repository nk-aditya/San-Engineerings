Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How long does a typical pool construction project take?

Project duration varies based on size and complexity, typically ranging from 2-6 months for residential pools and 4-8 months for commercial projects.

❓ Do you provide maintenance services after construction?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance contracts including annual servicing, pump maintenance, plumbing, electrical work, and 24/7 emergency support.

❓ What is the warranty on your work?

We provide comprehensive warranties on construction work and materials. Premium tiles come with lifetime warranty ensuring long-term durability and peace of mind.

❓ Do you work outside Delhi NCR?

Absolutely! We serve across North India including Gurugram, Noida, Meerut, UP, Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, and Uttrakhand. Contact us for your specific location.

Yes! We specialize in custom design solutions tailored to your unique requirements, aesthetic preferences, space constraints, and budget.
